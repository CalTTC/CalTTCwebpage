Meet the 2019-2020 Board Members!

The faces behind the club

Teddy Tran

President

I think it was a very interesting transition from competing against a lot of players at Cal before college to then becoming close friends and being on the same team and getting to know them better. I've played a lot of the players on the team competitively before, but I didn't get to know them until I came to Berkeley. And everyone has pretty diverse experiences and goals. Like, we actually have an EECS major who's taking EE courses. Incredible. Probably the only one on campus. And I think it's also really cool that our club is the cheapest sports club and we also take in anyone who wants to join.


Angela Guan

Vice President

I love how table tennis brings together so many diverse people who all love the sport and getting to know everyone! And to grow this sport I love.


Michael Whitmeyer

Social Committee Co-Chair

My friends! CalTTC has introduced me to some of my closest friends here at Cal, and every semester I get to meet new interesting people through CalTTC. My academic interests have come and gone, other clubs have come and gone, research has come and gone, and other friends have lost contact but CalTTC is FOREVER.
